Mumbai Game Library

 

Our Story so far...

 

 

Timeline
 Our Story
28-Jan-2018
 Launch our first social media account on Twitter.
27-Jan-2018
 After lots of feedback from involved participants and other people, we have decided to carry forward this tournament on PS4 platform.
Immediately taking feedback into consideration, we launch our 2nd FIFA18 Tournament on PS4 platform with
winning price of Rs.10,000/- Cash.
26-Jan-2018
 Successfully organised our first FIFA18 PC Tournament. There were total 32 participants took part and winner
received Rs.7000/- in Cash.
01-Jan-2018
 After successfully running library for 3 months, it came our mind that, not all people from Mumbai can afford expensive PS4 & Xbox1 games/consoles. This again leads to idea of providing rental facility of PS4 & Xbox1 gaming consoles. This way, every kid during their vacations can afford to play these next generation games at cheap rate.
03-Oct-2017
 Sold our first order containing FIFA18; which also turns out FIFA18 release date in India (although FIFA18 was released worldwide on 29-Sep-2017)
This leads to new idea of FIFA18 gaming tournament. So, we launch our first FIFA18 tournament. By doing so, FIFA18 unofficially became our brand ambassador game of us.
02-Oct-2017
 Launch Mumbai Game Library
On the day of Gandhi Jayanti, we launch online/remote game library for gamers in Mumbai who can buy or sell used games at cheap prices.
